Essel JKT

Pluit - +6282299007409

Essel JKT is a cozy restaurant located in the Muara Karang area of Pluit, North Jakarta, specializing in authentic Korean cuisine. The restaurant is known for its fresh, flavorful dishes, such as gimbap, Korean fried chicken, and other popular street food items. With a focus on quality ingredients and customer satisfaction, Essel JKT offers a welcoming atmosphere for dine-in customers and convenient options for takeout and delivery. Whether you're craving a quick snack or a full meal, Essel JKT provides a taste of Korea in the heart of Jakarta.

Popular dishes
Gimbap
Gimbap is a popular Korean dish made of steamed rice and various ingredients like vegetables, meat, and pickles, all rolled in seaweed (gim) and sliced into bite-sized pieces. It's often enjoyed as a snack or light meal.

Tteobeokki
Tteokbokki is a beloved Korean street food made from chewy rice cakes (tteok) cooked in a spicy and sweet gochujang-based sauce. It's often served with fish cakes, boiled eggs, and scallions, offering a rich, savory flavor with a kick of heat.
